Predicting the Oscars

CS152 Project Proposal

Project Description

I want to train neural networks (NNs) to predict the Academy Awards winners given the nominees for this year. I will specifically aim for predicting the "Best Picture" award by creating and using a dataset of previous nominees and winners and how they fared in earlier awards shows (e.g., SAGs, Golden Globes, BAFTA, etc.). I am not sure yet what else plays a role in who gets the Academy Award for "Best Picture," but I will look more into that, as a part of the initial/preparation stages of my project. I am thinking about testing the NN by feeding it data from 1947 to 2017 and making it "predict" the winners of 2018, 2019, and 2020 (which we already know). If the NN is able to predict at least one of them accurately, I will deploy it as an application and see what it predicts will be the winner this year (2021). If not, I will continue working on improving the model and the data that I am using for the predictions. This work can potentially be extended by training the NN to predict other categories, such as "Best Director," "Best Actor," "Best Actress," etc. (which will likely depend on a different set of data).

Project Goals

  1. Learn how voting works.
  2. Pick data to be used to predict "Best Picture."
  3. Scrape data for nominees and winners and make one comprehensive dataset.
  4. Successfully "predict" winners in the period 2018-2020.
  5. Predict the "Best Picture" winner for 2021.
